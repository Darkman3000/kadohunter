# Kill Criteria & Pivot Gates - `TripBrief`

> Rule: do not keep scaling a product that has already failed its own criteria. Re-evaluate at each milestone.

## D14 Milestone: Distribution & Interest

**Date:** `14 days after launch`

| Metric | Target | Minimum (Kill) |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| `Unique visitors` | `500` | `100` |
| `New accounts` | `50` | `10` |
| `First itinerary completions` | `20%` | `5%` |

**Action if Missed:**

- Status: `[KEEP / ITERATE / PIVOT / KILL]`
- Pivot: `change acquisition channel or simplify the first itinerary flow`

## D30 Milestone: Activation & Retention

**Date:** `30 days after launch`

| Metric | Target | Minimum (Kill) |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| `Week 1 retention` | `20%` | `5%` |
| `Repeat trip planners` | `25` | `5` |
| `Saved trip revisit rate` | `30%` | `10%` |

**Action if Missed:**

- Status: `[KEEP / ITERATE / PIVOT / KILL]`
- Iterate: `reduce planning friction and improve itinerary usefulness`

## D60 Milestone: Monetization & Viability

**Date:** `60 days after launch`

| Metric | Target | Minimum (Kill) |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| `Paid subscribers` | `15` | `1` |
| `Free-to-paid conversion` | `3%` | `0.5%` |
| `Monthly churn` | `20%` | `40%` |

**Action if Missed:**

- Status: `[KEEP / ITERATE / PIVOT / KILL]`
